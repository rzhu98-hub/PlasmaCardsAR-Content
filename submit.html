Submit a Reactor

Have a CAD model of a fusion machine? Get it into the deck — a new card and 3D model, live in the app without anyone updating anything.

How it works

  1. You send us a 3D model of the machine plus a bit of info (below).
  2. We review it — the maintainers check the model renders correctly, size it for AR, and design the trading card for it.
  3. It goes live on the content server. The 3D model appears in the app automatically. A brand-new card also needs the next app update to become scannable — models update instantly, cards ride app releases.

What to send

  • 3D model file: .obj (preferred), .fbx, .glb/.gltf, or .stl — under 50 MB. Include texture files if the model uses them (.mtl, images).
  • Machine facts: name, institution, real dimensions (approximate is fine), a 2–3 sentence description, and a link to the official page.
  • Optional: a nice photo or diagram for the card artwork, and how you'd like to be credited.
  • Please only submit models you have the right to share — your own work, your lab's (with permission), or openly licensed files.
