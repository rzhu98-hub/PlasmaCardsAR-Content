Step 1 — calibrate once

Do not pinch-zoom the page. Hold a real ruler against the blue bar and drag the slider until the bar is exactly 10 cm long.

Step 2 — pick a card

Card shows at true size: 12.7 × 7.14 cm. Tap left/right side to switch cards, tap top-left corner to come back. Use fullscreen (green window button) so nothing overlaps.
