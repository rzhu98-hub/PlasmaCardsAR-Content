The Reactors

How it works

  1. Print a plasma trading card (or grab one at an outreach event).
  2. Open the Plasma Cards AR app and point the camera at the card.
  3. The matching 3D reactor model appears, anchored to the card.
  4. Tap Save to My Library to keep it — then spin and zoom it any time, no card needed.

The models stream from this site, so new reactors appear in the app the moment they're published — no app update needed.

Print your own cards

Print the deck at exact physical size (12.7 × 7.14 cm — print at 100%, no scaling, and check the 10 cm ruler on each page), then scan the cards with the app.
